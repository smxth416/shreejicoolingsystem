

# Shreeji Cooling System Pvt. Ltd. — Corporate Website

## Overview
A modern, professional corporate website for an HVAC company showcasing their products, services, and expertise across commercial and industrial cooling solutions.

## Design Direction
- **Color palette**: Cool blues (#1e3a5f, #2980b9), steel greys, white backgrounds with blue accents
- **Style**: Clean, industrial-professional aesthetic with sharp lines, subtle gradients, and trust-building imagery
- **Typography**: Bold headings, clean body text — modern and corporate
- **Layout**: Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero section** with bold headline about cooling expertise, a CTA button ("Get a Free Quote"), and a background image/gradient
- **Service highlights** — 4 cards: Supply, Installation, Project Execution, Maintenance
- **Industries served** — Icons/cards for Malls, Offices, Factories, Large Stores
- **Why Choose Us** — Trust section with stats (years of experience, projects completed, clients served)
- **Client logos / testimonials** section
- **CTA banner** — "Need a cooling solution? Contact us today"

### 2. About Us
- Company story, mission, and vision
- Core values section
- Team / leadership overview
- Key milestones or timeline

### 3. Products
- **Category tabs/filters**: Air Conditioners, Chillers, Ductable/Ducted AC Systems, HVAC Equipment
- Product cards with image, name, short description, and "Enquire Now" button
- Each "Enquire Now" opens the inquiry form or scrolls to Contact

### 4. Our Services
- Dedicated sections for each service:
  - AC Installation
  - Chiller Systems
  - Ducting & HVAC Solutions
  - Project Execution
  - Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)
- Each with icon, description, and CTA

### 5. Blog
- Blog listing page with article cards (thumbnail, title, date, excerpt)
- Individual blog post detail page
- Placeholder content for a few sample articles (HVAC tips, energy saving, etc.)

### 6. Contact Us
- Inquiry form (Name, Email, Phone, Company, Message, Service Interest dropdown)
- Company address, phone, email details
- Embedded map placeholder
- Business hours

### 7. Career
- List of job openings with title, location, and short description
- "Apply Now" button opening a simple application form (Name, Email, Resume upload placeholder, Cover Letter)
- Sample job listings as placeholder data

### 8. CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility)
- Sections on Safety practices, Sustainability initiatives, and Responsible cooling
- Visual cards or timeline of CSR activities

---

## Global Elements
- **Header**: Logo, navigation menu (all 8 pages), "Get a Quote" CTA button, mobile hamburger menu
- **Footer**: Company info, quick links, contact details, social media icons
- **Floating "Free Inquiry" button**: Fixed on the right side of all pages — opens an inquiry modal form

---

## Technical Notes
- All content will be static (no backend needed) with placeholder data
- React Router for multi-page navigation
- Responsive design with Tailwind CSS
- Smooth scroll and subtle animations for a polished feel
